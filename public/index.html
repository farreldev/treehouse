Our Methodology

There are wide array of methodologies available to the researchers.

We are highly experienced in using multiple approaches to client’s problems, from quantitative to qualitative, both consumers direct and B2B approach.

We are trying to look at the client’s challenges from multiple angles and therefore we believe that employing multiple approaches is recommended.

Our approach to fieldwork and quality

We have a partnership with a fieldwork agency and an independent quality control agency.

Interviewers are managed by fieldwork agency with selection criteria set by Treehouse.

One interviewer can only do max 20% of the total sample. Interviews are checked by an independent QC agency (prior to interview for CLT, and post interview for D2D).

Treehouse have done a several Brand surveys, for Electronic Goods we have worked with both Japanese and Korean electronic goods companies in the past which, due to our confidentiality policy, cannot be revealed.
